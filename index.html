When you paste the code in, you will see this LS thing in the bottom part where you see the planes and stuff! Click that and pick what livery you want!

This is the pushback thing. At the bottom where the livery selector is, there will be the pushback thing, click on it and you'll see the pushback! You can control the speed and the way it turns!
